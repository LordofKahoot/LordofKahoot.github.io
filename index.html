The 1000 Yard Stair — Rocker-Bogie Stair Climber

Physics Analyst
Challenge
Design a ground-based robot to climb a 19-step staircase while transporting a water bottle, faster than competing teams.
Approach
Built a six-wheeled rocker-bogie chassis from laser-cut plywood, selected BRINGSMART 12V 27RPM worm gear motors via SolidWorks motion study (peak torque: 18 kg·cm), and designed custom 7-inch PETG wheels dip-coated in Plasti-Dip for rubber-on-concrete traction.
Result
Successfully climbed all 19 steps in 82 seconds with zero slip events, zero payload displacement, and zero chassis contact. Placed 2nd in the MAE 151B class competition.
